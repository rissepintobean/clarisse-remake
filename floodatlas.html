Houston Flood Atlas

print / map design / book design / collaboration

ˇ

This Atlas joins over twenty personal narratives chronicaling the unprecidented flooding in Houston brought by Hurricane Harvey. In collaboration with the Houston Flood Museum, each essay received an accompanying map. Muted tones, handmade textures, and sharp delicate lines were chosen to reflect the mood of the stories being depicted and demonstrating the idiosyncrasies that are inherent to living in a city like Houston, Texas. The Atlas is to be published in 2020.
