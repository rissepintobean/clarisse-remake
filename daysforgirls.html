Days for Girls Annual Report

print / book design

ˇ

Days for Girls is an organization bringing together grassroots groups to create reusable feminine hygiene solutions for school aged girls in impoverished areas around the world. The bold text and lines of stitching that break through the compositions reflect the idea of shattering stigmas and limitations, while also being a unifying element in its strength throughout the report.

The large text recedes into the background because while bold, it is the subtle strength of everyday living that is the focus of the project. The color palette is meant to be youthful and bright, while the image treatment places focus on the girls themselves.
