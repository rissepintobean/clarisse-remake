Reify is a student-run design shop aiming to bring greater attention to the University of Houston Graphic Design program, adding greater value to what is a hidden gem in the City of Houston. This desire is reflected in our name—Reify; to take the conceptual and make it real, to bring our concepts to life, to make our dreams a reality.
