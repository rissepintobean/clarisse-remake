Design Icon Site: Tibor Kalman

web design / type in motion / html / css

ˇ

The purpose of this site is to showcase the life and work of a chosen design icon, here Tibor Kalman. The look of the site is meant to reflect his philosophy for design, creating something traditional and breaking it just enough to keep people on their toes. Text animations in pure CSS are used to create simple impact without detracting from the showcased work that was originally created to speak for itself. Pulling directly from his manifesto, “culture used to be the opposite of commerce, not a fast track to ‘content’-derived riches.”
